Choosing between Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace is one of the most common technology decisions facing Mississauga small businesses today. Both platforms are cloud-based productivity suites that include email, file storage, video meetings, and collaboration tools. Both are used by millions of businesses worldwide. And both are genuinely excellent products.

So how do you choose?

The honest answer is that for most Mississauga SMBs, either platform will serve you well — the right choice depends on your existing workflows, your team's preferences, your industry's compliance requirements, and how you plan to use the tools.
