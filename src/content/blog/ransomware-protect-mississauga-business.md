Ransomware is the most financially damaging cyber threat facing Canadian small businesses today. In 2025, the Canadian Centre for Cyber Security reported that ransomware incidents cost Canadian organisations billions in ransom payments, recovery costs, and lost productivity. Small businesses — including many in Mississauga and the GTA — accounted for a significant share of that number.

If you have not thought about ransomware as a risk to your business, this guide is for you.

## What Is Ransomware?

Ransomware is a type of malicious software that encrypts the files on your computer and network, making them inaccessible. The attackers then demand a ransom payment — typically in cryptocurrency — in exchange for the decryption key. Without that key, your files are gone.
