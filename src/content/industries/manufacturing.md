### The Growing Cyber Threat to Manufacturing

Canadian manufacturing companies are increasingly targeted by ransomware attacks. Manufacturing is an attractive target for two reasons: the pressure to restore production quickly makes companies more likely to pay ransoms, and the convergence of IT and operational technology (OT) means a single attack can disable both business systems and production equipment simultaneously.

The Canadian Centre for Cyber Security has identified manufacturing as a critical infrastructure sector facing elevated cyber risk.
