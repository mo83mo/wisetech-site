## Why Mississauga Real Estate Professionals Choose WiseTech

Real estate is a relationship business driven by speed and trust. When technology fails — email goes down, a file won't open, a client portal crashes — it costs deals and client confidence. WiseTech ensures that the technology your Mississauga brokerage or property management company depends on works reliably, securely, and from wherever your team happens to be.

### The Mobile Reality of Real Estate

Real estate professionals spend more time outside the office than in it — at showings, listings, client meetings, and site visits. Your IT infrastructure must be designed for a mobile-first workforce, not a traditional office environment.

WiseTech configures Microsoft 365 and cloud phone systems so your agents can access email, files, and communication tools from any device, anywhere. Mobile device management ensures that company data on those devices is protected and can be remotely wiped if a device is lost or stolen.

### Wire Fraud: The Real Estate Industry's Biggest Cyber Threat

Real estate transactions involve large wire transfers, and this makes the industry a prime target for wire fraud. Cybercriminals monitor compromised email accounts for transaction communications and intercept them at the critical moment — substituting fraudulent wire instructions that redirect closing funds to criminal accounts.

Once funds are transferred, recovery is rarely possible. WiseTech protects your firm with email authentication controls (DMARC, DKIM, SPF) that prevent email impersonation, advanced filtering that detects suspicious communications, and security awareness training that teaches your agents and staff to verify wire instructions through a separate channel before sending funds.
