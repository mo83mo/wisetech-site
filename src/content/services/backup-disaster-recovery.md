### The Difference Between Backup and Disaster Recovery

Many businesses have some form of backup â€” an external hard drive, a basic cloud sync, or a manual copy to a USB drive. These are better than nothing, but they are not disaster recovery.

A proper disaster recovery strategy includes automated, encrypted, off-site backups with defined recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO), documented recovery procedures, and regular test restores to verify everything actually works.
