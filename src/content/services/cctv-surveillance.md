## Protect Your Mississauga Business with Professional Surveillance

A professionally installed CCTV system does more than record footage — it deters theft and vandalism, protects your employees, supports insurance claims, and gives you visibility into your business premises even when you are not on-site.

Off-the-shelf consumer camera systems from big-box stores are designed for homes, not businesses. They lack the reliability, image quality, storage capacity, and integration capabilities that commercial environments require. WiseTech designs and installs purpose-built IP camera systems that are engineered for business use.

### IP Cameras vs Analogue Systems

Modern IP (Internet Protocol) cameras deliver significantly better image quality than older analogue CCTV systems, with resolutions up to 4K and advanced features like intelligent motion detection, licence plate recognition, and facial detection. IP cameras connect to your business network, which means they can be managed remotely, integrated with other systems, and scaled by simply adding cameras to the network rather than running new coaxial cable throughout your building.

### Designed for Your Space

Every CCTV installation starts with a site survey. WiseTech visits your Mississauga premises, assesses your entry points, high-risk areas, and blind spots, and produces a camera placement plan that delivers complete coverage with the minimum number of cameras. We also consider lighting conditions, cable routing, and network infrastructure during the planning stage.

### Remote Monitoring and Alerts

WiseTech's CCTV systems include a mobile app for remote viewing from any smartphone or tablet. You can check in on your premises at any time, review footage for specific time periods, and receive motion-triggered alerts when activity is detected outside business hours.

### Integration with Access Control

For businesses that want a complete physical security solution, WiseTech integrates CCTV with electronic door access control. This allows you to correlate video footage with access events — seeing exactly who entered through which door and when — and to remotely lock or unlock doors from your smartphone.
