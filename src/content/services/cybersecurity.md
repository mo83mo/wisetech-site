## Why Mississauga Businesses Need Cybersecurity

A cyberattack is no longer an "if" — it's a "when." Canadian small businesses are hit with ransomware, phishing, and data breaches every day, and the consequences are severe: the average cost of a ransomware incident for a Canadian SMB is between $50,000 and $200,000 when you factor in downtime, recovery, regulatory fines, and reputational damage.

The good news is that the vast majority of cyberattacks are preventable with the right defences in place.

### The Layered Security Approach

No single tool stops every threat. WiseTech deploys a layered security stack that covers every attack vector:

**Endpoint protection** ensures that every device — workstations, laptops, and servers — is running advanced EDR software that detects and responds to threats in real time. Unlike traditional antivirus, EDR identifies unusual behaviour patterns that signature-based tools miss.

**Email security** is critical because over 90% of successful cyberattacks start with a phishing email. Our email filtering solution blocks malicious attachments, impersonation attempts, and suspicious links before they reach your employees' inboxes.

**Human training** closes the gap that technology alone cannot. Our quarterly security awareness training teaches your team to recognise phishing emails, suspicious links, and social engineering tactics — turning your people from your biggest vulnerability into a genuine line of defence.

### PHIPA and PIPEDA Compliance

Businesses in regulated industries — dental offices, law firms, accounting practices — have legal obligations around data security under PHIPA and PIPEDA. WiseTech's Advanced plan includes compliance documentation and reporting to help you meet these requirements and qualify for cyber insurance at favourable rates.
