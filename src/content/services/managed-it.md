## Why Mississauga Businesses Switch to Managed IT

Most small business owners in Mississauga didn't start their company to manage IT problems. Yet the average SMB loses 12 to 16 hours of productivity per year to IT downtime — hours that cost real money and frustrate your team.

Managed IT services change the model entirely. Instead of waiting for something to break and then scrambling to fix it, WiseTech monitors your entire IT environment around the clock and resolves most issues before your team even notices them.

### What "Proactive" Actually Means

When WiseTech manages your IT, we install lightweight monitoring software on every workstation and server in your office. This software alerts our technicians the moment it detects an anomaly — a hard drive showing early failure signs, a server running too hot, a workstation that hasn't received security patches. We fix these problems in the background, often outside business hours, so your team stays productive.

### Flat Monthly Pricing — No Surprise Invoices

Break-fix IT support creates a misaligned incentive: the less your IT company fixes proactively, the more you pay them when things go wrong. Managed IT flips this. At WiseTech, we make more money when your IT runs well, so we have every reason to keep it that way.

Your monthly fee covers unlimited helpdesk support, all monitoring, patch management, and vendor coordination. No per-ticket charges, no hourly billing for routine work, no invoice shock.

### Built for Mississauga SMBs

WiseTech is a local Mississauga MSP. Our technicians are familiar with the GTA's business environment, can be on-site quickly when needed, and understand the specific challenges facing small businesses in industries like legal, healthcare, accounting, and manufacturing.

Whether you have five employees or fifty, we right-size your IT support so you're not paying for enterprise features you don't need — and not going without protections you do.
