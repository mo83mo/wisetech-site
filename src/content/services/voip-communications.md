## Why Mississauga Businesses Are Switching to VoIP

Traditional business phone systems — physical PBX hardware, landlines, and legacy carriers — are expensive to maintain, difficult to scale, and completely unsuited to a workforce that increasingly works from multiple locations or remotely.

VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) replaces your physical phone infrastructure with a cloud-based system that delivers better features, greater flexibility, and significantly lower costs.

### The Business Case for VoIP

The cost savings alone make a compelling case. Most Mississauga SMBs save 30–50% on monthly phone costs when they switch from a traditional system to VoIP — through lower call rates, elimination of PBX hardware maintenance contracts, and the ability to manage the entire system through a web portal rather than calling a technician every time you need to add a line.

Beyond cost, VoIP gives your business capabilities that traditional phone systems cannot match: voicemail delivered to email, auto-attendant that routes calls professionally 24/7, call recording for compliance and training, and a softphone app that lets any team member take their business number with them on their laptop or mobile.

### Built for a Flexible Workforce

The modern Mississauga business does not operate entirely from one location. Team members work from the office, from home, from client sites, and on the road. VoIP ensures your phone system follows your people rather than tying them to a desk.

Every user gets a business number and extension that works on their office phone, their laptop, and their mobile device. Calls can be transferred between devices mid-conversation, and the caller always sees your professional business number — not a personal mobile.
